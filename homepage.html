Our History

It all started in a small garage with nothing but a second-hand roaster and a passion for the perfect bean. What began as a personal quest to find the richest flavors of East Africa and Central America soon turned into a mission to share those discoveries with our neighbors.

In 2015, we opened our first doors here in Rades, dedicated to the belief that coffee should never be rushed. Over the years, our space has changed, but our method hasn't: we still roast in small batches, we still pour by hand, and we still treat every guest like the very first person who walked through our doors a decade ago. This is our history, and every cup we serve is a new chapter.
